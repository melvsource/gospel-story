Better Understanding of the Gospel Story

See the Bible as one unified narrative, not disconnected events.

Most Christians read the Bible in fragments — a verse here, a passage there — without ever seeing the thread that runs from Genesis to Revelation. This book traces that thread: the one Gospel Story that holds all of Scripture together, centered on the person and work of Jesus Christ.

How the Gospel shapes everyday life, identity, and discipleship.

Understanding the Gospel Story is not merely an intellectual exercise — it reshapes how we live. This book explores what it looks like when the Gospel becomes the lens through which a believer sees their identity, their relationships, and their calling in the world.
