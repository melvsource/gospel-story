# The Gospel in One Sentence

If you had to summarize the entire Gospel in one sentence, what would you say?

## The Attempt

Paul does it in Romans 1:16 — *"It is the power of God for salvation to everyone who believes."* Short. Dense. Loaded with meaning.

## Why This Matters

We live in an age of summaries. But the Gospel resists reduction — not because it is complicated, but because it is inexhaustible. Every attempt to compress it reveals something true, and leaves something else waiting to be said.

> The Gospel is not a formula. It is a Person.

## Where to Go From Here

Read the Gospel of John in one sitting. Let the story carry you rather than stopping at every verse. See if, by the end, you can write your own sentence.
