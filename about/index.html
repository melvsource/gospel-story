The Mission

About

Why The Gospel Story exists, and who is behind it.

✦

I write to help believers and seekers understand Scripture through the Gospel Story.

Rather than focusing on isolated passages or individual themes, my goal is to help readers see the Bible as one coherent revelation — a single, unbroken narrative that begins in Genesis, finds its center in Jesus Christ, and reaches its completion in Revelation.

Many people read Scripture faithfully for years without ever seeing the thread that runs through all of it. The sermons, studies, and books on this site exist to make that thread visible.

Convictions

What I Believe About Scripture

  • The Bible is one book, not a library of unrelated texts. Every part points toward Christ.
  • The Old Testament is not merely background — it is the first half of the story, without which the New Testament cannot be fully understood.
  • The Gospel is bigger than personal salvation. It is the announcement that God is restoring all things through His Son.
  • Scripture is best understood when read as its authors intended — as a continuous narrative with a coherent plot, real characters, and a definitive resolution.
